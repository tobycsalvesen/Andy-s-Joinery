About Andy

I'm Andrew, a freelance joiner and carpenter based in the Scottish Borders with over 20 years of experience delivering bespoke, high-quality work. Whether it’s a custom designed kitchen, a set of garden gates, or carefully crafted wardrobes — I take pride in every detail. I believe great craftsmanship starts with good relationships, honest work, and making sure every project feels like it matters. Let’s build something together.
